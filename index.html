Clinical Biochemistry

Clinical Biochemistry & Immunology provides qualitative and quantitative analysis of biological fluids such as blood, serum or plasma, urine, or tissues for specific chemical constituents or physiologic processes. Majority of routine checkups are powered by these tests.

Molecular Biology

Various techniques are used to analyze biological markers in the genome & proteome—by applying molecular biology to medical testing. Our offerings include platforms like liquid biopsies, PCR, rtPCR, qPCR, to name a few. Molecular Biology is the future of predicting chronic illness at an early stage.

Hematology

Cell counting, cell sorting, biomarker detection & protein engineering. Our advanced platform allows us to do extremely high quality reporting for all kinds of diseases including very specialised panels for Minimum Residual Disease monitoring.

Microbiology

Our Microbiology department encompasses numerous sub-disciplines including virology, parasitology, mycology and bacteriology.

Serology and Immunology

Our Clinical immunology department specializes in diseases caused by disorders of the immune system and diseases of other systems, where immune reactions play a part in the pathology and present clinical features.

Clinical Pathology

Our clinical pathology division relies on superior automation to attain highly accurate and dependable test results leading to faster turn around times for our referring clinicians.
